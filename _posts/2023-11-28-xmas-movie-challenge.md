It must be Italian! â€” Old Man Parker, A Christmas Story" %} Spanning all genres they bring us warmth and laughter, tears, frights, and joy.  The 25-day Christmas Movie Challenge is an opportunity to share your favorites starting on December 1st leading up to Christmas day. There are 25 prompts, one for each day. How do we play? 

## Playing the Game

1. For each prompt, pick a movie.
2. Make a social media post, we prefer [Instagram](https://instagram.com).
3. Share your post with hashtag: __#25daychristmasmoviechallenge__
